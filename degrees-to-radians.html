Common Angle Conversions

0° = 0 rad
30° = π/6 rad ≈ 0.524 rad
45° = π/4 rad ≈ 0.785 rad
60° = π/3 rad ≈ 1.047 rad
90° = π/2 rad ≈ 1.571 rad
180° = π rad ≈ 3.142 rad
270° = 3π/2 rad ≈ 4.712 rad
360° = 2π rad ≈ 6.283 rad

Conversion Formulas

Degrees to Radians:

radians = degrees × (π / 180)

Radians to Degrees:

degrees = radians × (180 / π)
